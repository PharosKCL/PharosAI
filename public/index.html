About us

PharosAl is a partnership between King's College London, Barts Cancer Institute and two NHS trusts, revolutionising Al-assisted healthcare. We aim to build high-quality, enriched patient datasets and educate the workforce for Al-enabled care. Your support will significantly accelerate our mission to deliver the right treatment, at the right time, for all patients, regardless of their socioeconomic background..
